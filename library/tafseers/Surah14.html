As-salamu alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatohu

Start your Day by reading Quran: (1709)

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

الۤرۚ كِتَـٰبٌ أَنزَلۡنَـٰهُ إِلَیۡكَ لِتُخۡرِجَ ٱلنَّاسَ مِنَ ٱلظُّلُمَـٰتِ إِلَى ٱلنُّورِ بِإِذۡنِ رَبِّهِمۡ إِلَىٰ صِرَ ٰ⁠طِ ٱلۡعَزِیزِ ٱلۡحَمِیدِ.

(Surah Ibrahim: 1)

Main Topics of Surah:

Quran Khuda ki Kitab,

Kuffar ki shanakht,

Har Nabi apni Qaum ki zaban mein Tableegh karta hai,

Bani Israel ka haal,

Shukr se Ne'mat aue Nashukri se Azaab mein izaafa hota hai,

Ahle Imaan ko Khuda par bharosa karna chahiye,

Kalma e Tayyaba ki Tashbeeh aur Kalma e Khabisha ki Tashbeeh,

Allah Ahle Imaan ko Saabit Qadam rakhta hai,

Kufran e Ne'mat ka Natija,

Khuda ki Ne'maat ka shumaar na-mumkin hai,

Hazrat Ibrahim (as) ki Dua,

Makkah mein Hazrat Ismael (as) ki sukoonat,

Khuda ko har shakhs ke Amaal ki khabar hai,

Qayamat ka Zikr aur

Khuda Waada Khilaafi nahi karta.

As-salamu alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatohu

Start your Day by reading Quran: (1710)

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

الۤرۚ كِتَـٰبٌ أَنزَلۡنَـٰهُ إِلَیۡكَ لِتُخۡرِجَ ٱلنَّاسَ مِنَ ٱلظُّلُمَـٰتِ إِلَى ٱلنُّورِ بِإِذۡنِ رَبِّهِمۡ إِلَىٰ صِرَ ٰ⁠طِ ٱلۡعَزِیزِ ٱلۡحَمِیدِ.

📚

Surah Ibrahim ka aghaaz karte hue Allah Ta'ala ne Qur'an e Majeed ki ye Sifat bayan ki hai ke ye Kitab jahan Nabuwwat e Mustafa (saww) ka zinda javed Mo'jeza hai, wahin ye Zulmaat (Darkness) se nikaal kar Roshni mein laane wali bhi hai. Ye Azeem Kitab Ghalib o Layeq Khuda ke Raaste ki Rahnumayi karti hai.

Allah Ta'ala ne pehli Ayat mein Apne Aap ko "Aziz Hameed" keh kar logon ke Zehno ko is haqeeqat ki taraf mutawajjah kiya hai ke tumhe jis Khuda ki Raah ki Dawat di ja rahi hai Woh Khuda aisa Ghalib hai jo kabhi Maghloob nahi ho sakta aur Woh Ghani e Mutlaq hai, kisi ka Mohtaj nahi hai, aur Apni Makhlooq par Faizaan e Karam karne wala hai. Lehaza jab Ina'am dene wala Ghalib o Ghani hai to Ina'am haasil karne walon ko bhi chahiye ke woh Uski Dawat par Labbaik kahen taake Uske mazeed Ehsanaat ke qabil ban jayen aur Uski Nafarmani se ehtraaz karen kyonki jahan Woh Rehman o Rahim hai, wahin Woh "Shadeedul Eqaab" bhi hai aur agar Woh chahe to Zameen ke waasiyon ko khatm kar ke doosri Makhlooq ko bhi is Zameen par abaad kar sakta hai aur Usne aisa kai baar kiya bhi hai. Aasmano aur Zameen ki Ayaat "Afaaq o Anfus" is haqeeqat ki shahid hain ke tamam Ne'maat ka sar chashma (source) Wohi hai aur Wohi Layeq e Hamd hai aur Uske alawa koi Ma'bood nahi hai.

Start your Day by reading Quran: (1711)

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

الۤرۚ كِتَـٰبٌ أَنزَلۡنَـٰهُ إِلَیۡكَ لِتُخۡرِجَ ٱلنَّاسَ مِنَ ٱلظُّلُمَـٰتِ إِلَى ٱلنُّورِ بِإِذۡنِ رَبِّهِمۡ إِلَىٰ صِرَ ٰ⁠طِ ٱلۡعَزِیزِ ٱلۡحَمِیدِ.

"Alim Laam Raa! Ye ek aisi Kitab hai jise Hamne tumhari taraf nazil kiya hai, taake tum logon ko unke Parwardigar ke Hukm se Tareekiyon se nikaal kar Roshni mein laayen. Us Khuda ke Raaste par jo Ghalib hai aur Layeq e Hamd hai."

(Surah Ibrahim: 1)

https://youtu.be/hP1d2RvuigE

📚📖

👉 Tafseer:

📚

Allah Ta'ala ne Be'sat e Rasool ka buniyadi Maqsad ye bayan kiya hai ke Aap (saww) is Kitab ke zariye se logon ko Tareekiyon se nikaal kar Roshni mein le aayen. Qur'an Majeed mein Rasoole Khuda (saww) ki ye Zimmedari bayan ki gayi hai ke Aap (saww) Bani No' Insaan ko Tareekiyon se nikaal kar Roshni mein le aayen. Aap (saww) ki Nabuwwat ka dayra (circle) sirf Aap (saww) ki Qaum o Qabile tak mehdood nahi hai.

Allah Ta'ala ne Nuzoole Qur'an ki Hikmat bayan karte hue farmaya:

"Woh Zaat Sahib e Barkat hai, Jisne Apne Bande par Qur'an nazil kiya ke woh tamam Jahano ke liye Daranewaale bane." (Furqan: 1)

🕯️

Allah Ta'ala ne Aap (saww) se farmaya:

"Aap (saww) keh den ke aye Insaano! Mein tum sab ki taraf Allah ka Rasool hun." (Araaf: 158)

    ♦️ DUNIYAVI MUHABBAT ♦️

As-salamu alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatohu

Start your Day by reading Quran: (1712)

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

ٱللَّهِ ٱلَّذِی لَهُۥ مَا فِی ٱلسَّمَـٰوَ ٰ⁠تِ وَمَا فِی ٱلۡأَرۡضِۗ وَوَیۡلࣱ لِّلۡكَـٰفِرِینَ مِنۡ عَذَابࣲ شَدِیدٍ * ٱلَّذِینَ یَسۡتَحِبُّونَ ٱلۡحَیَوٰةَ ٱلدُّنۡیَا عَلَى ٱلۡـَٔاخِرَةِ وَیَصُدُّونَ عَن سَبِیلِ ٱللَّهِ وَیَبۡغُونَهَا عِوَجًاۚ أُو۟لَـٰۤىِٕكَ فِی ضَلَـٰلِۭ بَعِیدࣲ.

"Woh Khuda ke Aasmaano aur Zameen ki tamam cheezen jis ki Milkiyat hain aur jo Sakht Azaab Munkarin e Haq ke liye tayyar kiya gaya hai, Uski wajah se unke Halakat hai."

"Woh jo Duniyavi Zindagi ko Akherat par tarjeeh dete hain aur woh Allah ke Raaste se rok rahe hain aur woh us mein kaji (tedhapan) paida karne ki Khwahishmand hain. Yahi log parle darje ki (extreme) Gumrahi mein hain."

(Surah Ibrahim: 2 - 3)

https://youtu.be/hP1d2RvuigE

📚📖

👉 Tafseer:

https://shiachannel.in/library/tafseer.html?id=1

📚

Ye poori Kaynaat Allah ki Milkiyat hai, hamara kuchh bhi nahi. Ham to is Duniya mein kuchh din ke liye hain. Lekin ye Kafir nahi samajhte, Duniya ki Muhabbat mein ye sab bhool gaye hain. Inhe Akherat par eteqaad nahi. Isliye ye log Deendar logon ko uncultured, Backward samajhte hain.

Inki 3 khusoosiyat is Ayat mein bayan ki gayi hain:

1. Woh Allah ka Raasta rokte hain. Anti-Muslim Strategy.

2. Ye Nek kaamo ko bigadne mein lage hain. Yani woh log chahte hain ke Musalman naam ke rahen, Islami Rooh ghayeb rahe. E.g. Namaz padhen, Sadqa den lekin dikhave ke liye, Quran ka Qiraat Competition ya Ghar ke rasmon mein istemal ho, padhen lekin uske maano par tawajjo na den etc.

3. Ye log aisi Gumrahi mein door chale gaye hain ke in par koi Tableegh o Nasihat asar nahi hogi.

   ♦️ ZABAAN-E-ANBIYA (as) ♦️

As-salamu alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatohu

Start your Day by reading Quran: (1713)

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

وَمَاۤ أَرۡسَلۡنَا مِن رَّسُولٍ إِلَّا بِلِسَانِ قَوۡمِهِۦ لِیُبَیِّنَ لَهُمۡۖ فَیُضِلُّ ٱللَّهُ مَن یَشَاۤءُ وَیَهۡدِی مَن یَشَاۤءُۚ وَهُوَ ٱلۡعَزِیزُ ٱلۡحَكِیمُ.

"Hamne jis bhi Rasool ko bheja to uski Qaum ki Zabaan de kar hi bheja, taake woh achhi tarah se unhe khul kar samjhaye. Phir Khuda jise chahta hai Gumrahi mein chhod deta hai aur jiske liye chahta hai use Hidayat karta hai aur Wohi Ghalib aur Hikmatwala hai."

(Surah Ibrahim: 4)

https://youtu.be/hP1d2RvuigE

📚📖

👉 Tafseer:   (Cont'd)

https://shiachannel.in/library/tafseer.html?id=1

📚

Is Topic par guzishta Ayat mein zikr ho chuka hai aur mazeed jari rakhte hain:

👉🏻

Allah Ta'ala ki ye Sunnat rahi hai ke jis Paighambar ko kisi bhi ilaaqe mein mab'oos kiya to woh apne Mukhtebeen e Awwalin ki zabaan mein bolta tha. Agar Allah kisi doosri zabaan bolne wale ko Nabi bana kar bhejta to log kehte ke hame to uski zabaan hi samajh mein nahi aati. Ham us par Imaan laayen to akhir kaise laayen?

Rasoole Khuda (saww) agarche tamam Insaano ke Rasool hain, Aap (saww) ki Dawat tamam aqwaam ke liye hai, iske bawajood Allah ne Aap (saww) par Arabic Zaban mein Qur'an nazil kiya aur Aap (saww) khud bhi Arabic zaban bolte the, iski wajah ye thi ke Aapki Dawat ke pehle Mukhatab Arab hi the. Agar bilfarz Qur'an e Hakeem China zaban mein nazil hota to Ahle Arab ise samajh hi na sakte.

📚

Rasoole Khuda (saww):

"Allah Ta'ala ne mujh par Apna khusoosi karam kiya aur mujh se farmaya: 'Aye Muhammad (saww)! Meine har Rasool ko uski Qaum ki Zabaan de kar uski Qaum ke liye mab'oos kiya aur Meine tujhe tamam Ahmar o Aswad (Red & Black) ke liye mab'oos kiya."

(Kitabul Khisaal, Noorus)

📚

Imam Sadiq (as):

"Allah Ta'ala ne Anbiya (as) par jo bhi Wahy ki wo Arabi Zabaan mein hi thi albatta jab Anbiya ke Kaano mein Wahy pahuchti thi to unki Qaum ki Zabaan mein hi unhe sunayi deti thi jabke hamare Rasool ko Arabic mein hi sunayi deti thi aur AanHazrat (saww) se agar koi ghair Arab apni Zabaan mein guftugu karta tha to Jibrael Amin (as) uski guftugu ka Arabic mein Tarjuma karke Aapko usi waqt hi suna dete the. Ye Allah ki taraf se Aapke liye khusoosi ezaaz tha."

(Ilalush Sharaye, Noorus Saqlain)

📚

Rasoole Akram (saww) tamam Insaano, Ahle Mashriq o Maghrib aur tamam Ahle Arz o Sama ke liye Rasool the. Aap (saww) ne poori Zindagi Makkah o Madina mein guzari to tamam logon ko kab tableegh ki?

Is sawal ke jawab mein Imam Sadiq (as) ne farmaya:

"Allah Ta'ala ne Jibrael Amin (as) ko Hukm diya to us ne poori Zameen utha kar AanHazrat (saww) ke saamne pesh ki aur AanHazrat (saww) ne tamam Aqwaam o Milal ko Islam ki Dawat di aur har Qaum se unki Zabaan mein guftugu ki."

     ♦️ KUFRAAN-E-NE'MAT ♦️

As-salamu alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatohu

Start your Day by reading Quran: (1718)

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

قَالَتۡ رُسُلُهُمۡ أَفِی ٱللَّهِ شَكࣱّ فَاطِرِ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَ ٰ⁠تِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِۖ یَدۡعُوكُمۡ لِیَغۡفِرَ لَكُم مِّن ذُنُوبِكُمۡ وَیُؤَخِّرَكُمۡ إِلَىٰۤ أَجَلࣲ مُّسَمࣰّىۚ قَالُوۤا۟ إِنۡ أَنتُمۡ إِلَّا بَشَرࣱ مِّثۡلُنَا تُرِیدُونَ أَن تَصُدُّونَا عَمَّا كَانَ یَعۡبُدُ ءَابَاۤؤُنَا فَأۡتُونَا بِسُلۡطَـٰنࣲ مُّبِینࣲ *  قَالَتۡ لَهُمۡ رُسُلُهُمۡ إِن نَّحۡنُ إِلَّا بَشَرࣱ مِّثۡلُكُمۡ وَلَـٰكِنَّ ٱللَّهَ یَمُنُّ عَلَىٰ مَن یَشَاۤءُ مِنۡ عِبَادِهِۦۖ وَمَا كَانَ لَنَاۤ أَن نَّأۡتِیَكُم بِسُلۡطَـٰنٍ إِلَّا بِإِذۡنِ ٱللَّهِۚ وَعَلَى ٱللَّهِ فَلۡیَتَوَكَّلِ ٱلۡمُؤۡمِنُونَ.

"Unke Rasoolon ne kaha: 'Kya tumhe Aasmaano aur Zameen ke Khaliq Khuda mein Shak hai? Woh tumhe Apni taraf Dawat de raha hai, taake Woh tumhare Gunah Ma'af kare aur tumko ek muqarrarah waqt tak Mohlat de.' Woh kehne lage ke tum bhi to hamare hi jaise Insaan ho. Tum hame un ma'boodon se rokna chahte ho jinki Ibaadat hamare Aaba o Ajdaad kiya karte the, tum Uske liye hamare paas koi sareeh (clear) Mo'jeza lao."

"Unke Rasoolon ne unse kaha ham bhi tumhare jaise hi Insaan hain lekin Khuda Apne Bandon mein se jis par chahta hai Ehsaan karta hai aur hamare ikhtiyar mein nahi hai ke ham Hukm e Khuda ke baghair tumhare saamne koi Mo'jeza pesh karen aur Ahle Imaan ko Khuda par hi Bharosa rakhna chahiye."

(Surah Ibrahim: 10 - 11)

👉 Tafseer: (Cont'd)

🎋🍂🍃🌾

Jaisa ke Guzishta Ayaat mein bhi zikr ho chuka hai ke Insaani Fitrat is baat se inkaar nahi kar sakti ke uska koi Khaliq hai, balke Woh poori Kaynat ka Khaliq hai. Is mein koi shak ki baat hi nahi. Lekin phir bhi woh Inkaar karte hain. Rasool Allah ka bheja hua Numainda hota hai, jisko logon ki Hidayat ke liye bheja gaya hai. Usko bhi ye ham jaise Insaan samjhte hain. Us waqt ki baat nahi balke aaj bhi Musalmanon ka ek tabqa yehi kehne laga ke Rasool (saww) to ham jaise Insaan hain, is mein Fazilat ki kya baat hai. Ye Muqassir hain inka maqsad inki Fazilat ko girana aur be qadri karna hai. Jaisa ke hamne bataya ke Khuda ka Rasool bhejna bhi hamare liye ek Ne'mat hai. Inka Inkaar karna Kufraane Ne'mat hai. Usi tarah Rasoole Khuda (saww) ke baad Allah ke muntakhab Aimma (as) ka bhi Kufraan e Ne'mat hai. In Wahabiyon ko Allah ne doosri Ayat mein jawab diya hai.

Insaan ki jis cheez ki adat pad jati hai uska chhodna mushkil ho jata hai, yehi hal un Kafiron ka tha. Unke Abaa o Ajdaad Buton ki Pooja karte aaye the to unka apne is reeti riwaj ko chodna mushkil tha. Woh Nabi se zyada apne Baap-Dada ko ahmiyyat de rahe the. Yani woh apni Aql ka istemal nahi karte the. Is wajah se woh Gunah pe Gunah karte jaa rahe the aur Allah bhi mohlat deta hai taake Tauba kar ke Raahe Raast par aa jayen. Unke saamne itne Mo'jezaat runuma ho rahe the lekin apni hatdharmi mein ade hue the.

وَمَا لَنَاۤ أَلَّا نَتَوَكَّلَ عَلَى ٱللَّهِ وَقَدۡ هَدَىٰنَا سُبُلَنَاۚ وَلَنَصۡبِرَنَّ عَلَىٰ مَاۤ ءَاذَیۡتُمُونَاۚ وَعَلَى ٱللَّهِ فَلۡیَتَوَكَّلِ ٱلۡمُتَوَكِّلُونَ.

       ♦️ PADOSI - NEIGHBOUR ♦️

As-salamu alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatohu

Start your Day by reading Quran: (1720)

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

وَقَالَ ٱلَّذِینَ كَفَرُوا۟ لِرُسُلِهِمۡ لَنُخۡرِجَنَّكُم مِّنۡ أَرۡضِنَاۤ أَوۡ لَتَعُودُنَّ فِی مِلَّتِنَاۖ فَأَوۡحَىٰۤ إِلَیۡهِمۡ رَبُّهُمۡ لَنُهۡلِكَنَّ ٱلظَّـٰلِمِینَ * وَلَنُسۡكِنَنَّكُمُ ٱلۡأَرۡضَ مِنۢ بَعۡدِهِمۡۚ ذَ ٰ⁠لِكَ لِمَنۡ خَافَ مَقَامِی وَخَافَ وَعِیدِ.

"Aur Kafiron ne apne Rasoolon se kaha: 'Ham zaroor tumhe apni sar zameen se nikal denge ya ye ke tum hamari Millat mein wapas aa jao. Unke Rab ne unki taraf Wahy farmayi ke Ham Zalimon ko zaroor halaak karenge, aur unki halakat ke baad tumhe Zameen par basayenge. Ye Ina'am uske liye hai jo Mere Huzoor Jawabdahi ka khauf rakhta ho aur meri Waeed (Threat) se darta ho."

(Surah Ibrahim: 13 - 14)

https://youtu.be/hP1d2RvuigE

📚📖

👉 Tafseer:

https://shiachannel.in/library/tafseer.html?id=1

🕌

Kafir chunke Us Khuda ki Qurat par yaqeen nahi rakhte the to is tarah ki baaten kar rahe the.

Aaj bhi Insaan ka jab kisi se jhagda hota hai to sarkashi karne wala bahut hekdi jatata hai jaise ke 'Jaanta nahi mein kaun hun, ye kar kar dunga, wo kar dunga,' jabke kar kuchh nahi sakta. Agar do padosi apas mein jhagda karen, un mein se ek zara zabar aur sarkash ho, dono kiraye par hon. Zabar wala koshish karega ke usko yahan se nikalwa de. Lekin hoga ulta. Insaafwar Makan malik us Sarkash Zalim ko apne ghar se nikal kar doosre Kirayedar ko, jo achha hoga,wohi ghar de dega.

Is Zameen ka Malik Allah hai, ham to waqtiya reh rahe hain. Ab Jahil Kafir jinki Aqlon mein Patthar pade hain, Rasoolon se ye kahen ke ham tumhe nikal denge, to Allah iske jawab mein kehta hai tum kya nikaloge ham tumhe hi nikal denge aur unki zameen unko hi de denge.

As-salamu alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatohu

Start your Day by reading Quran: (1722)

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

وَٱسۡتَفۡتَحُوا۟ وَخَابَ كُلُّ جَبَّارٍ عَنِیدࣲ.

"Paighambaron ne Faisle ki darkhwast ki (to Faisla ye hua ke) Sarkash Adawat Rakhnewala Halaak hua."

(Surah Ibrahim: 15)

https://youtu.be/hP1d2RvuigE

📚📖

👉 Tafseer:

https://shiachannel.in/library/tafseer.html?id=1

🕌

Rasoole Khuda (saww):

"Aye Ali! Tumhare andar Esa bin Maryam (as) ki Mushabehat (Similarity) paayi jaati hai. Agar mujhe ye andesha na hota ke log tere mutalliq wohi baaten na karne lag jaayen jo Nasara Hazrat Maseeh (as) ke mutalliq karte hain to mein tere itne Fazail bayan na karta ke jahan se tera guzar hota log tere Qadmon ki Mitti ko Tabarruk samajh kar uthane lag jaate."

AanHazrat (saww) ka ye farman kuchh Arabiyon ko sun kar giran guzra to ye Ayat nazil hui:

وَلَمَّا ضُرِبَ ٱبۡنُ مَرۡیَمَ مَثَلًا إِذَا قَوۡمُكَ مِنۡهُ یَصِدُّونَ

"Jab Ibne Maryam (as) ki misal bayan ki jaati hai to Aap (saww) ki Qaum usse Munh mod leti hai." (Zokhrof: 57)

Haris bin Umar Al Fehri ne ye suna to usne kaha:

"Khudaya! agar ye baat Haq hai to ham par Aasmaan se Pattharon ki Baarish nazil kar ya ham par dardnak Azaab bhej de."

Allah Ta'ala ne is Sawal ke Jawab mein ye Ayat nazil farmayi:

"Jab tak Aap (saww) unke darmiyan maujood hain us waqt tak Khuda un par Azaab nazil nahi karega." (Anfal: 32)

AanHazrat (saww) ne usse farmaya ke tu Tauba karta hai ya yahan se jata hai?

Fehri ne kaha:

"Muhammad (saww)! Aapne to Arab o Ajam ki saari Fazilaten Bani Hashim mein jama kar di hain. Aap Quraish ko bhi iska hissedar banayen."

AanHazrat (saww) ne farmaya:

"Ye mere ikhtiyar mein nahi hai."

Doosre is tarah  *وَلِوَ ٰ⁠لِدَیَّ* (Waladayya) padhenge to matlab hoga 'Meri Aulaad'. Jab Qur'an nazil hua tha to Eraab Zer Zabar, nuqte nahi the, likhne ka dastoor kam tha. Quran Mo'meneen ke zehen mein mehfooz tha. Isliye is mein tabdeeli ke imkaan ho sakte hain. Ham dono maani le sakte hain.

Agar Hazrat Ibrahim (as) ne apne Walid ke liye Dua ki to unhone apne asl Waalid ke liye Dua ki. Azar ke liye Quran mein *اب* (Ab) istemal hua hai. 'Ab' Chacha aur Baap do ke liye istemal hota hai. Kafir ke liye kisi Nabi ne Dua nahi ki.

Doosre maani ke mutabiq Hazrat Ibrahim (as) ne apne Farzandon Ishaaq (as) aur Ismael (as) ke liye Dua ki.

😏

Kisi ke Makr aur Chaal se woh dhoka khata hai jise Makr aur Chaal ka Ilm na ho jabke Khuda ko har ek ki Chaal ka pehle se Ilm hai. Lehaza Kafir jitni bhi Chaalen kyon na chalen woh Khuda ko aajiz nahi kar sakte. "Pahad hat sakna" ek Mohavera hai. Ye unki Chaal ki gambhirta ko bata raha hai.

Is zimn mein kuchh Mufassirin ne daur e Namrood ka ek waqea naql kiya hai ke usne chaar Gidhon گدھ (Vulture) ko pakad kar khoob pala posa. Jab wo parwaz ke qabil ho gayin to usne ek Sandooq tayyar kiya aur Sandooq ki har taraf se ek ek Gidh ko bandha aur ek shakhs ko Sandooq mein baithaya aur kaha ke tum inhe gosht sikhate jana, ye Aasmaanon ki taraf  parwaz karengi. Tum upar ja kar Kaynat ki haqeeqat ko dekh kar aao. Phir aa kar hame bhi usse ba khabar karo. To usne waisa hi kiya, upar se Aasmaan waisa ke waisa hi dikhayi diya aur Zameen ki taraf dekha to andhera hi andhera dikhayi diya. Woh parwaz karte hue niche aaye. Jab Pahadon ne Gidhon ki is udan ko dekha to qareeb tha ke woh apni jagah se hat jayen. Is riwayat ya kahani se ye andaza lagaya ja sakta ke Pahad ka hatna ye ek Mohavera hai, baat mein wazan dalne ke liye ye istemal kiya jata hai.
