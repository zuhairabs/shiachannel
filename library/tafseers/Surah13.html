As-salamu alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatohu

Start your Day by reading Quran: (1655)

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

الۤمۤرۚ تِلۡكَ ءَایَـٰتُ ٱلۡكِتَـٰبِۗ وَٱلَّذِیۤ أُنزِلَ إِلَیۡكَ مِن رَّبِّكَ ٱلۡحَقُّ وَلَـٰكِنَّ أَكۡثَرَ ٱلنَّاسِ لَا یُؤۡمِنُونَ.

"Alim, Laam, Meem, Ra! Ye Kitabe Ilahi ki Ayaat hain, aur jo kuchh tumhare Rab ki taraf se tum par nazil kiya gaya hai wo Ain e Haq hai. Magar aksar log maan nahi rahe hain."

(Surah Raa'd: 1)

----------------------------------------------

MORAL OF THE DAY ....!!!!!!!!

🙇 📚📖

👉 Tafseer:

Aaj se ham Surah Raa'd ki shuru'at kar rahe hain.

🕋

Surah Raa'd Makkah mein nazil hua magar iski do Ayaat Madine mein nazil hui.

📚📖

Imam Sadiq (as):

"Jo shakhs kasrat se Surah Raa'd ki Tilawat kare to us par Aasmaani Bijli kabhi nahi giregi khwah woh Nasibi hi kyon na ho. Agar koi Momin iski ba kasrat Tilawat kare to woh Hisaab ke baghair Jannat mein dakhil hoga aur use uske Khandan aur Bhaiyyon ke mutalliq Haqqe Shafa'at diya jayega."

(Kitab Sawabul Amaal, Noorus Saqlain)

📚📖

Rasoole Khuda (saww):

"Jo koi Surah Raa'd ki Tilawat kare to aaj tak Duniya mein jitne bhi Badal guzar chuke hain aur Qayamat tak jitne bhi Badal uthenge use unki miqdar ke barabar Sawab diya jayega aur Qayamat ke Din Ahde Khuda Wandi ke tahet use Amn naseeb hoga."

(Majmaul Bayan, Noorus Saqlain)

📚📖

*Is Surah ke kuchh Topics:*

Qur'an, God-The Creator, Qaadir, Aalim, Har Cheez Khuda ki Tasbeeh karti hai, Har Qaum ka ek Hadi, Farishton ka Protection, Tuba, Anbiya ki mutaddid Biwiyan thi, Hazrat Ali (as) ki Fazilat.

🤲🏻

يَا مَنْ يُّسَبِّحُ الرَّعْدُ بِحَمْدِهِ،.....

"Aye Woh jiski Raa'd (Badlon ki garaj & lightening) tasbeeh karte hain."

(Dua-e-Iftetah)

   ♦ HUROOFE MUQATTA'AT♦

As-salamu alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatohu

Start your Day by reading Quran: (1656)

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

الۤمۤرۚ تِلۡكَ ءَایَـٰتُ ٱلۡكِتَـٰبِۗ وَٱلَّذِیۤ أُنزِلَ إِلَیۡكَ مِن رَّبِّكَ ٱلۡحَقُّ وَلَـٰكِنَّ أَكۡثَرَ ٱلنَّاسِ لَا یُؤۡمِنُونَ.

"Alim, Laam, Meem, Ra! Ye Kitabe Ilahi ki Ayaat hain, aur jo kuchh tumhare Rab ki taraf se tum par nazil kiya gaya hai wo Ain e Haq hai. Magar aksar log maan nahi rahe hain."

(Surah Raa'd: 1)

----------------------------------------------

MORAL OF THE DAY ....!!!!!!!!

🙇 📚📖

👉 Tafseer: 

Huroof e Muqatta'at ke bare mein guzishta Suron mein zikr kar chuke hain.

📚

Imam Sadiq (as):

"Ayat الۤمۤرۚ ka mafhoom hai:

اِنَّا اللّٰهَ المُحْيِى المٌمِيت الرَّازِق.

"Mein Allah hi Hayat Denewala, Maut Denewala aur Rizq Denewala hun."

Imam Baqir (as):

"Abi Lubaid! Allah Ta'ala ne Qur'an Majeed ke Huroofe Muqatta'at mein meree liye bahut zyada Ilm rakha hai. Allah ne 'Alif Laam Mim' ki Ayat nazil ki. Iske tahet Hazrat Mohammad Mustafa (saww) ne qayam kiya, yahan Aapka Noor zahir hua aur Aapki baat qaem hui jis Din Aapki Wiladat hui to us waqt Satveen Alif mein se 103 Saal guzar chuke the."

"Is wazahat Kitab e Khuda ke Huroof e Muqatta'at mein maujood hai. Jab tum Huroof e Muqatta'at ko repeat kiye baghair shumar karoge to iske ikhtetam par Bani Hashim mein se koi na koi khada hone wala qayam karega."

"Alif ka Adad  1 hai. Laam ke Adad 30 hain, Mim ke 40 aur Saad ke adad 90 hain, ye Total 161 bante hain. Imam Husain (as) ke qayam ki ibteda bhi "Alim Laam Mim" se hui. Jab iski muddat poori hui to "Alif Laam Mim Saad" ke tahet Bani Abbas mein qayam karne wale ne qayam kiya. Phir jab "Alif Laam Raa" ke saath uska khatma ho gaya to hamara Qaem (atfs) qayam karega."

"Abi Lubaid! Ise achhi tarah se samjho aur yaad rakho aur ise chhipa kar rakho."

(Kitab Ma'anil Akhbar, Noorus Saqlain)

🤲🏻

اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَى الْاَعْضَاءِ الْمُقَطَّعَاتِ،.....

"Salaam ho un bikhre hue Aaza par,...."

(Ziyarat-e-Nahiya)

As-salamu alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatohu

Start your Day by reading Quran: (1657)

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

ٱللَّهُ ٱلَّذِی رَفَعَ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَ ٰ⁠تِ بِغَیۡرِ عَمَدࣲ تَرَوۡنَهَاۖ ثُمَّ ٱسۡتَوَىٰ عَلَى ٱلۡعَرۡشِۖ وَسَخَّرَ ٱلشَّمۡسَ وَٱلۡقَمَرَۖ كُلࣱّ یَجۡرِی لِأَجَلࣲ مُّسَمࣰّىۚ یُدَبِّرُ ٱلۡأَمۡرَ یُفَصِّلُ ٱلۡـَٔایَـٰتِ لَعَلَّكُم بِلِقَاۤءِ رَبِّكُمۡ تُوقِنُونَ * وَهُوَ ٱلَّذِی مَدَّ ٱلۡأَرۡضَ وَجَعَلَ فِیهَا رَوَ ٰ⁠سِیَ وَأَنۡهَـٰرࣰاۖ وَمِن كُلِّ ٱلثَّمَرَ ٰ⁠تِ جَعَلَ فِیهَا زَوۡجَیۡنِ ٱثۡنَیۡنِۖ یُغۡشِی ٱلَّیۡلَ ٱلنَّهَارَۚ إِنَّ فِی ذَ ٰ⁠لِكَ لَـَٔایَـٰتࣲ لِّقَوۡمࣲ یَتَفَكَّرُونَ.

"Allah, wohi to hai jisne Aasmaano ko aise saharon ke baghair buland kiya, jo tumko nazar aate hon, phir Arsh e Iqtedar par Jalwa Afoz hua, aur Usne Suraj aur Chaand ko ek Qanoon ka paband banaya. Is saare Nizaam ki har cheez ek waqt e muqarrar tak ke liye chal rahi hai aur Allah hi is saare kaam ki tadbeer farma raha hai. Woh Ayaat ko khol kar bayan kar raha hai taake tumko aa jaye."

"Aur Wohi hai Jisne ye Zameen phaila rakhi hai, us mein Pahadon ke Khoonte gaad rakhe hain, aur Darya baha diye hain. Usne har tarah ke Phalon ke Jode paida kiye hain. Aur Wohi Din par Raat taari karta hai. In saari cheezon mein badi Nishaniyan hain un logon ke liye jo ghaur o fikr se kaam lete hain."

(Surah Raa'd: 2 - 3)

----------------------------------------------

MORAL OF THE DAY ....!!!!!!!!

🙇 📚📖  ☁️

👉 Tafseer:

Is Ayat mein Allah ne hamari Fikron ko dawat di ke dekhen ye sab kisne paida kiya?

☁️

Imam Reza (as) se Ayat (51:7) ka matlab daryaft kiya to Aap (as) ne farmaya:

"Khuda farma raha hai mujhe Aasmaan ki Qasam jis mein Raaste hain."

Phir Aap (as) ne apni Ungliyon ko ek doosre mein paiwast kar ke kaha: "Zameen o Aasmaan is tarah muttasil (connected) hain."

Raavi ne poochha ke magar Allah Ta'ala ne to farmaya ke Aasmaan baghair Pillars ke hain. (13:2)

Imam (as) ne farmaya:

"Is Ayat mein Allah ne Sutoonon (Pillars) ki nafi nahi ki hai balke tumhare dekhne ki nafi ki hai jabke Aasmaan Ghair Marayi (Invisible) Sutoonon par qaem hai."

(Tafseer Ali Ibne Ibrahim Qummi, Noorus Saqlain)

☁️☁️

Nahjul Balagha mein Hazrat Ali (as) ke ye Kalemaat hain:

"Uski takhleeq ke shawahed mein kisi Sutoon aur sahare ke baghair mazboot Aasmaan ki takhleeq shamil hai."

"Neeche wale Aasmaan ko ruki hui Mauj ki tarah banaya aud upar wale Aasmaan ko mehfooz Chhat aur buland Imaarat ki tarah banaya. Uske liya na to Sutoono ki zaroorat mehsoos hui aur na kisi bandhan se jodne ki."

(Nahjul Balagha, Noorus Saqlain)

🌚❄️

Imam Sadiq (as):

"Jab Aankh nigah karti hai to use mukhtalif Makhlooq dikhayi deti hai jo ek doosre se muttasil hai. Dil isse istidlal karta hai ke is Makhlooq ka koi na koi Khaliq hai, kyonki jab Aankh Aasmaan ki bulandi aur baghair kisi Sutoon ke use Hawa mein buland dekhti hai to Dil ye istidlal karta hai ke Aasmaan ek jagah par qaem hain. Ye aage peechhe nahi ho rahe, warna toot jate aur un mein harkat nahi hai warna apne maqam se hat jaate aur neeche nahi hote ke qareeb ho jayen aur buland nahi hote ke dikhayi na den."

(Kitab Ahleelja, Noorus Saqlain)

🤲🏻

يَا مَنِ الظُّلْمَةُ عِنْدَهُ ضِيَاۤءٌ اَسْاَلُكَ بِنُوْرِ وَجْهِكَ الْكَرِيْمِ الَّذِيْ تَجَلَّيْتَ بِهِ لِلْجَبَلِ فَجَعَلْتَهُ دَكًّا وَ خَرَّ مُوْسٰى صَعِقًا وَ بِاسْمِكَ الَّذِيْ رَفَعْتَ بِهِ السَّمَاوَاتِ بِلَا عَمَدٍ وَ سَطَحْتَ بِهِ الْاَرْضَ عَلٰى وَجْهِ مَاۤءٍ جَمَدٍ وَ،.....

(Dua e Arafa wa Shabe Juma - Mafateeh)

Start your Day by reading Quran: (1658)

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

وَفِی ٱلۡأَرۡضِ قِطَعࣱ مُّتَجَـٰوِرَ ٰ⁠تࣱ وَجَنَّـٰتࣱ مِّنۡ أَعۡنَـٰبࣲ وَزَرۡعࣱ وَنَخِیلࣱ صِنۡوَانࣱ وَغَیۡرُ صِنۡوَانࣲ یُسۡقَىٰ بِمَاۤءࣲ وَ ٰ⁠حِدࣲ وَنُفَضِّلُ بَعۡضَهَا عَلَىٰ بَعۡضࣲ فِی ٱلۡأُكُلِۚ إِنَّ فِی ذَ ٰ⁠لِكَ لَـَٔایَـٰتࣲ لِّقَوۡمࣲ یَعۡقِلُونَ.

"Aur dekho, Zameen mein alag alag khitte paye jate hain jo ek doosre se jude hue hain hain. Angoor ke Baug hain, Khetiyan hain, Khajoor ke Darakht hain, jin mein se kuchh ikehre aur kuchh dobre (do shakhen), jabke sabko ek hi Paani se seraab karta hai, magar zayqe mein kisi ko Ham kisiko behtar bana dete hain aur kisi ko kamtar. In sab cheezon mein bahut si Nishaniyan hain un logon ke liye jo Aql o Sha'oor se kaam lete hain. "

(Surah Raa'd: 4)

----------------------------------------------

MORAL OF THE DAY ....!!!!!!!!

🙇 📚📖  ☁️

Is Ayat mein Allah ne hamari Fikron ko dawat di ke dekhen ye sab kisne paida kiya? Ye bhi guzishta Ayat se marboot hai.

Is Ayat ka maqsad ye hai ke Kadwi Zameen Meethi Zemeen ke qareeb hai, lekin har ek ki taseer juda juda hai, jis tarah se log ek doosre ke qareeb hote hain lekin har ek ki Tabiyat aur Fitrat juda juda hoti hai.

(Tafseer e Ayyashi, Noorus Saqlain)

Is ke maani 'Misaal' ke bhi hain. Jaise Rasoole Khuda (saww) ne farmaya: عم الرجل صنوانيه

("Chacha Baap ki Shabeeh hota hai.")

🌳🌴

"Log mukhtalif Shajron se talluq rakhte hain jabke mein aur tu ek hi Shajre se talluq rakhte hain." Phir AanHazrat (saww) ne ye Ayat tilawat farmayi (13:4)

(Majmaul Bayan, Noorus Saqlain)

         ♦ MAUT O HAYAT ♦

As-salamu alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatohu

Start your Day by reading Quran: (1659)

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

وَإِن تَعۡجَبۡ فَعَجَبࣱ قَوۡلُهُمۡ أَءِذَا كُنَّا تُرَ ٰ⁠بًا أَءِنَّا لَفِی خَلۡقࣲ جَدِیدٍۗ أُو۟لَـٰۤىِٕكَ ٱلَّذِینَ كَفَرُوا۟ بِرَبِّهِمۡۖ وَأُو۟لَـٰۤىِٕكَ ٱلۡأَغۡلَـٰلُ فِیۤ أَعۡنَاقِهِمۡۖ وَأُو۟لَـٰۤىِٕكَ أَصۡحَـٰبُ ٱلنَّارِۖ هُمۡ فِیهَا خَـٰلِدُونَ.

"Ab agar tumhe tajjub karna hai to tajjub ke qabil logon ka ye qaul hai ke, "Jab mar kar ham mitti ho jayenge to kya ham naye sire se paida kiye jayege?" Yeh woh log hain Jinhone apne Rab se Kufr kiya hai, ye woh log hain jinki gardano mein Tauq pade hue hain. Ye Jahannami  hain, aur Jahannum mein hamesha rahenge."

(Surah Raa'd: 5)

🙇 📚📖  

👉 Tafsir:

Insaan apni Zindagi mein Allah ki Nishaniyan dekhta rehta hai, un mein se ek Nishani Maut hai. Koi Insaan aisa nahi jo kisi ke Janaze mein sharik na hua ho. Ye Janaza har uske uthane wale ko sabaq deta jata hai ke aapko bhi is Mitti mein aana hai.

Atheist ye samajhte hai ke Admi ke marne par sab kuchh khatm ho jata hai. Ye Kufr hai. Kafir ye tajjub karta hai ke kaise mumkin hai ke ek Insaan jo Mitti mein khatm ho gaya use Zinda uthaya jayega? Quran mein kai jagah par is sawal ka jawab dohraya hai. Aisa sawal hi uski Tauheed ki kamzori hai. Insaan ye nahi sochta ke mein kaise wujood mein aa gaya.

وَضَرَبَ لَنَا مَثَلࣰا وَنَسِیَ خَلۡقَهُۥۖ قَالَ مَن یُحۡیِ ٱلۡعِظَـٰمَ وَهِیَ رَمِیمࣱ * قُلۡ یُحۡیِیهَا ٱلَّذِیۤ أَنشَأَهَاۤ أَوَّلَ مَرَّةࣲۖ وَهُوَ بِكُلِّ خَلۡقٍ عَلِیمٌ

"Insaan apni Paidaish ko bhool jata hai aur kehta hai: "Kaun in Haddiyon ko Zinda karega jabke ye bosida ho chuki hain."

Unse keh do unhe Wohi Zinda karega Jisne pehle unhe Paida kya tha."

(Yaseen: 78 - 79)

Taheed ye hai ke Insaan apne Khaliq ko pehchane. Wohi Paida Karnewala aur Maut Denewala hai. Insaan ki Zindagi bas Yaume Paidaish o Maut ka darmiyani hissa hai faqat. Ye aisi cheezen hai jiska Insaan khud mushaheda karta hai, isliye inkar ka koi sawal hi nahi uthta. Insaan ke liya agar ye masla hal ho gaya to baqi sab mamle bhi hal ho jayenge.

(Man La Yahzarahul Faqih, v1, p101)

(From the book Sahifa Sajjadiya)

Jab Suraj ki tez shu'ayen Samandaron aur Jheelon ki satah se Bukharat (Vapours) uthati hain to woh Bukharat jo nanhi nanhi boondon ka majmua hota hain. Baadlon ki dilfareb surat mein fiza mein lehrane aur Hawa mein udne lagte hain aur jab Hawa ke jhonke unhe harkat mein laate hain to unki tahon mein jama shuda zakhira kabhi halki halki phuar aur kabhi dhuan dhar Baarish ki surat mein barasne lagta hai aur Teelon Chattano par se guzarta Nadi Naalon ko chhalakta hai, Zameem ke zarre zarre ko seraab kar deta hai, jisse Zameen ki satah par haryali aur Kashtkar ke chehre par surkhi daud jati hai. Chunanche Khuda Wande Alam ka irshaad hai:

"Khuda Woh hai jo Hawa ko chalata hai to Woh Baadlon ko harkat mein laati hai. Phir Woh jis tarah chahta hai unhe fiza mein phailata hai aur inhe tukde tukde kar deta hai. Phir tum dekhte ho ke Baadlon ke  andar se boonde nikal padti hain, phir Khuda apne Bandon mein jis par chahta hai unhe barsa deta hai to wo khushiyan manane lagte hain." (Rome:48)

Jab un Baadlon mein nanhi nanhi boonden Hawa se takrati ya aapas mein ragad khati hain to us takrao se un mein Barqi Quwwat (Electric Force) paida hoti hai jo baaz Baadlon mein Musbat (Positive Pole) aur jis taraf ko jati hai use (Negative Pole) keh lete hain. Jab ye Musbat +ve aur Manfi - ve wale Badal ek doosre ke qareeb aate hain ye dono mutazad (opposite) quwwaqten aapas mein takrati hain jisse Roshni ka ek Sharara (Arc) paida hote hai jo apni tezi aur chamak ki wajah se Ankhon mein chaka chond paida kar deta hai. Us Sharare ka naam Barq (Light) hai. Ye Barq har waqt darakhshan rehti hai aur ek second mein kam o besh 100 martaba chamakti hai aur uske har Sharare mein 10 Crore Volt se le kar 20 Arab Volt tak Bijli hoti hai. Us Sharara se is qadar garmi (Heat) paida hoti hai ke aas paas ki Hawa garam ho jati hai aur us garmi ki wajah se uska phailao badh jata hai aur uski jagah par charon taraf se thandi Hawain intihai tez raftar ke saath badhti hain jisse kadak ki awaaz paida hoti hai. Us kadak ka naam *رعد* Raa'd hai. Ye kadak Bijli ke chamakne ke chand daqeeqe (Milli Second) ke baad sunai deti hai. Is liye ke Awaaz ki raftar Roshni ki raftar se sust hoti hai.

Chunache Roshni ki raftar 1,86,284 Mile per second hai aur Awaaz ki raftar 370 Gaz per second hai. Is lehaz se agar ek Mile ke faasle se Roshni aur Awaaz ek saath chalen to Awaaz 5 second baad pahunchegi. Kabhi kabhi ye Bijli Zameen par gir bhi padti hai. As-salamu alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatohu

Start your Day by reading Quran: (1685)

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

جَنَّـٰتُ عَدۡنࣲ یَدۡخُلُونَهَا وَمَن صَلَحَ مِنۡ ءَابَاۤىِٕهِمۡ وَأَزۡوَ ٰ⁠جِهِمۡ وَذُرِّیَّـٰتِهِمۡۖ وَٱلۡمَلَـٰۤىِٕكَةُ یَدۡخُلُونَ عَلَیۡهِم مِّن كُلِّ بَابࣲ * سَلَـٰمٌ عَلَیۡكُم بِمَا صَبَرۡتُمۡۚ فَنِعۡمَ عُقۡبَى ٱلدَّارِ.

"Yani aise Baugh jo unki Abadi Qayamgah honge, woh khud un mein dakhil honge aur unke Abaa o Ajdaad aur unki Biwiyon aur Aulad mein se jo bhi  Saleh hon woh unke saath wahan jayenge, Malaika har Darwaze se unke istiqbal ke liye ayenge. Sabr karne walon! Tum par Salam ho, Akherat ka Ghar kitna hi achha hai."

(Surah Raa'd: 23 - 24)

📚📖 🕌

👉 Tafseer:  (Cont'd)

https://shiachannel.in/library/tafseer.html?id=1

Jab Ahle Aql (Intelligent) Jannat e Adn mein dakhil honge to Malaika unka Welcome karenge.

🕌

Khuda phir ek Farishte se Jannat ki Mubarak dene ke liye rawana karega. Woh Uski Hoor se Shaadi karenge. Jab woh uske Sadr Darwaze par pahunchenge to woh uske Darban Farishte se kahenge ke Khuda se pyaron se hamare liye ijazat hasil karo, kyonki hame use Mubarak baad pesh karna hai.

Darban kahega: 'Mein tumhara ye Paigham Uske Hajib tak pahuncha dunga. Darban aur Hajib ke darmiyan ka faasla 3 Bhaghaat ka hoga. Darban Farishta ijazat talab karega. Hajib kahega ke ijazat mushkil hai kyonki is waqt woh apni Biwi ke saath hai. Baher haal tumhara Paigham pahuncha dunga. Hajib aur Allah ke Pyaron ka faasla bhi do Baagh ka hoga. Hajib unke Nigran Khaas ko Paigham dega. Nigran Khaas Paigham le kar Khuddam ke paas jayega. Khuddam ko ijazat mil jayegi. Iske Mehel ke ek Hazar Darwaze honge. Har Darwaze se ek Farishta dakhil hoga aue use Mubarak baad dega aur woh Khuda ki taraf se use Salaam ka Tohfa pesh karke kahenge. (13:24) "Tumhare Sabr karne ki wajah se tum par Salamti ho, Akherat ka Ghar kitna hi achha hai."

(Rauza e Kaafi, Tafseer Ali Ibne Ibrahim Qummi, Noorus Saqlain)

🕌

Imam Sadiq (as):

"Malaika ke ek giroh ne Bani Adam par Halal Lazzat o Shehwat ke mutalliq etraaz kiya. Allah ko Malaika ki ye Tanqeed pasand na aayi. Allah ne etraaz karne walon mein Lazzat o Shehwat ka maddah paida kar diya, taake woh Ahle Imaan par etraaz na kae saken. Jab Malaika ne apne andar is maddeh ko mehsoos kiya to unhone Khuda ke Huzoor Faryaad ki ke Parwardigar, ham Ma'afi chahte hain. Hamen hamari pehli Fitrat ki taraf lauta de. Agar ye maddah ham mein maujood raha to ham pareshani mein mubtila ho jayenge.

Allah ne un se Lazzat o Shehwat ka maddah khatm kar diya. Jab Qayamat ka Din hoga aur Ahle Jannat Jannat mein jayenge to wohi Malaika Ahle Jannat se mulaqat ki ijazat talab karenge aur jab unhe ijazat mil jayegi yo woh unhe Salaam karke kahenge: 'Tum par Salaam ho, tumne Duniya mein reh kar Halal Lazzat o Shehwat par Qana'at ki thi."

(Tafseer Ayyashi, Noorus Saqlain)

     ♦️ ALLAH RAAZIQ HAI ♦️

As-salamu alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatohu

Start your Day by reading Quran: (1687)

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

ٱللَّهُ یَبۡسُطُ ٱلرِّزۡقَ لِمَن یَشَاۤءُ وَیَقۡدِرُۚ وَفَرِحُوا۟ بِٱلۡحَیَوٰةِ ٱلدُّنۡیَا وَمَا ٱلۡحَیَوٰةُ ٱلدُّنۡیَا فِی ٱلۡـَٔاخِرَةِ إِلَّا مَتَـٰعࣱ.

"Allah jise chahta hai Rizq badhata hai ghatata hai. Aur ye log Duniyavi Zindagi mein mast ho gaye hain, halanke Duniya Akherat ke muqabile mein nihayat (haqeer) Poonji hai."

(Surah Raa'd: 26)

https://youtu.be/hP1d2RvuigE

📚📖 💰

👉 Tafseer:

https://shiachannel.in/library/tafseer.html?id=1

Jaisa ke guzishta Ayat mein 3 tarah ke logon par Allah ne La'anat ki hai, woh Ahle Aql (Intelligent) nahi ho sakte. Is Ayat mein inhi logon ko mukhatib kiya hai ke ye log Duniyavi Zindagi ko Value dete hain, isliye aisi harkate karte hain. Har shakhs Duniyavi Rizq ke liye daud raha hai lekin kisko kitna Rizq milna hai ye Allah ke Ikhtiyar mein hai. Rozi ka badhana ghatana Allah ke ikhtiyar mein hai. Insaan ka kaam sirf koshish karke hasil karna hai. Waise bhi ye Duniya Akherat ke muqabile mein koi haisiyat nahi rakhti.

Lekin Duniyavi Zindagi ke liye kasbe Ma'ash (Livelihood) bhi zaroori hai. Rozi talash karna Insaan par Farz hai. Kaahil, Sust logon ki Allah ne Mazammat ki hai.

       ♦️ ITMINAN-E-QALB ♦️

As-salamu alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatohu

Start your Day by reading Quran: (1688)

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

وَیَقُولُ ٱلَّذِینَ كَفَرُوا۟ لَوۡلَاۤ أُنزِلَ عَلَیۡهِ ءَایَةࣱ مِّن رَّبِّهِۦۚ قُلۡ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ یُضِلُّ مَن یَشَاۤءُ وَیَهۡدِیۤ إِلَیۡهِ مَنۡ أَنَابَ *  ٱلَّذِینَ ءَامَنُوا۟ وَتَطۡمَىِٕنُّ قُلُوبُهُم بِذِكۡرِ ٱللَّهِۗ أَلَا بِذِكۡرِ ٱللَّهِ تَطۡمَىِٕنُّ ٱلۡقُلُوبُ.

"Aur Kafir kehte hain ke is Paighambar par uske Rab ki taraf se koi Nishani kyon na utri.' Aap (saww) keh den:' Khuda jise chahta hai Gumrahi mein rakhta hai aur jo Uski taraf rujoo kare to use Apni jaanib Rahnumayi karta hai."

"Aur jo log Imaan laye hain aur unke Dil Allah ke Zikr se Itminan paate hain. Aagah raho ke Dilo ko Yaade Khuda se hi Itminan naseeb hota hai."

(Surah Raa'd: 27 - 28)

📚📖

👉 Tafseer:

Rasoole Khuda (saww) ne Ali (as) se farmaya:

"Ya Ali! 3 kaam aise hain jinki is Ummat mein taqat nahi hai: 'Maal mein Deeni Bhaiyyon ke saath Mawasaat (equality) karna, apni taraf se logon ka Haq ada karna, aur har haalat mein Khuda ko yaad rakhna lekin Khuda ki Yaad sirf' Subhanallah wal Hamdo Lillah wa La Ilaha Illallaho Wallaho Akbar nahi hai balke Yaad e Khuda ye hai ke jis waqt Insaan kisi Fe'l e Haram ka saamna kare to Khuda se Dare aur use Tark kar de."

(Safeenatul Bihar, Vol-1, P-484)

🕌

Hazrat Ali (as):

الذکر ذکر اللہ عزو جل عند المصیبة و افضل من ذٰلک ذکر اللہ عند ما حرم اللہ علیک فیکون حاجزاً۔

"Zikr do qism ke hain. Ek to Khuda ko Musibat ke waqt Yaad rakhna, (aur Sabr o Isteqamat se kaam lena) aur isse afzal o bartar ye hai ke Muharramat (Harams) ke muqabile mein Khuda ko Yaad rakha jaye aur iske aur Haram kaam ke darmiyan Diwaar khadi kar di jaye."

(Safeenatul Bihar, Vol-1, P-484)

🕌

"Apne liye Sipar muhayya karo."

(Safeenatul Bihar, Vol-1, P-484)

🕌

Imam Sadiq (as):

بمحمد تطمئن القلوب وھو ذکر اللہ و حجابہ

"Muhammad (saww) ke zariye Dilon ko sukoon hota hai. Wo hai Khuda ka Zikr aur Uska Hijaab."

(Tafseere Namoona)

🕌

Ek taraf to Allah Ta'ala farma raha hai ke Allah ke Zikr se Dilon ko sukoon milta hai, lekin doosri Ayat mein Allah Ta'ala farmata hai:

انما المومنون الذین اذا ذکراللہ وجلت قلوبھم

"Momin woh hai ke jis waqt Khuda ka Zikr kiya jaye to unka Dil dhadakne lagta hai."

Kya ye dono Ayaton mein ikhtelaf, contradiction hai?

Iske wujuhaat bahut hain. In short ye ke Insaan ka Khuda ka Zikr karne se us mein Umeed o Raja' paida hoti hai. Allah par jab Tawakkul karta hai to Dundiya ka Khauf, Pareshani, Bala Musibaten uske liye kuchh maana nahi rakhti. Woh apni Zindagi Allah ke Bharose chhod deta hai.

Doosri Ayat mein ye hai ke jab Insaan Allah ka Zikr karta hai, Uski Azmat, Kibriyai ko dekhta hai, apni Zimmedariyon ko mehsoos karta hai, tab uska Dil dhadakne lagta hai.

Bada' بداء Kya hai?

Riwayat e Masoom (as) hai:

مَا عَرَفَ اللّٰهَ حَقَّ مَعرِفَتِهِ مَن لَم يَعرُفُهٗ بِالبَدَاءِ

Ye khaalis Shia Aqeeda hai.  Baqi firqe is par Aqeeda nahi rakhte. Sunni Mufassir Fakhre Raazi ne ise Baatil qarar diya hai. Lekin usko nahi maloom ke ye Aqeede ka sar chashma yehi Ayat 'Mahv o Isbaat' hai jisko hamne tafseel se zikr kiya.

Bada' yaani Ashkaar hona, zahir hona, poori tarah se waazeh hona. Aur iske maani Pasheman hone ke bhi hain.

Jaise ke pehle wazeh kar chuke hain Allah ke paas Ummul Kitab hai aur hamare paas Ilmul Kitab. Ilmul Kitab par hawadis, Amal asarandaz hote hain. Mulaheza farmayen:

1. Hazrat Yunus (as) ki Qaum par Azaab aate aate tal gaya jab unki Qaum ne khuloos e Dil se Tauba ki. Azaab ka talna Bada waaqe hona hai. (Yunus: 98)

2. Hazrat Esa (as) ne jaate jaate predict kiya tha ke ye Dulhan mar jayegi aur Wedding ceremony sog mein badal jayegi, lekin jab wapsi mein dekha to aisa nahi hua. Jab unse poochha gaya to bataya ke unhone Sadaqa diya tha. Sadaqa badi se badi Balaon ko taal deta hai.

(Biharul Anwaar, Vol 2 (old), Amaali Sudooq)

3. Hazrat Ibrahim (as) ka Hazrat Ismail (as) ko Zabah ke liye tayyar hona aur unki jagah ek Dumba zabah hona. Ye bhi Bada' waaqe hua.

4. Hazrat Musa (as) ne apni Qaum se 30 Din bataye aur aaye 40 Din baad. Ye bhi Bada' hai.

In sab waqe'aat se ye Natija nahi nikalna chahiye ke ham samjhe Allah ko pehle Ilm nahi tha baad mein pata chala. Allah ko sab pata hai,  Usko ye bhi pata hai ke pehle kya batana hai aur baad mein kya hona hai. Ye sab Allah ki Maslehat hai. Kisi ka Imtihan leta hai, azmata hai, to kisi ko mohlat deta hai. Chahe aam Insaan hon ya Anbiya wa Aimma hon. Imtihan sabka liya jaata hai. Lekin level alag alag hote hain. Means Ilmul Kitab ki har maloomat mein 'if' (Agar), 'then' (to phir) laga hota hai. Har Ilm shartiyah hota hai. Aur iska talluq Aalam e Tashrii' se hai, Aalame Takveen Allah ke Ikhtiyar mein hai. Zahoor e Imam Mehdi (atfs) Takveeni hai yani hoke rahega is mein Bada' nahi lekin iska waqt ka talluq Taahrii' se hai. Jaise Hazrat Musa (as) apni muddat ke pehle apni Ummat mein zahir ho gaye unki Ummat ki kaseer Duaon ki wajah se, yani Bada' hua.

Baaqi tafseel Kitabon mein darj hai, refer karen.

Imam Baqir (as):

(Rauzatul Waezeen Mufeed, Basaerud Darajat)

📚

Imam Sadiq (as) apne Ashaab ke darmiyan mein baithe farma rahe the:

"Un logon par intihai tajjub hai jo hamare mutalliq ye guman karte hain ke ham Ghaib jaante hain jabke Allah ke alawa Ghaib ka Ilm kisi ke paas nahi hai. Meine apni Kaneez ko saza dena chahi to woh bhag gayi. Mujhe to ye pata nahi hai ke woh ghar ke kis kone ke kamre mein chhupi hui hai."

Ashaab ne kaha:

"Ham aap par qurban jaye, aaj Aap (as) ne Kaneez ke mutalliq ye jumle irshad farmaye jabke hame pata hai ke Aap (as) bahut kuchh jaante hain magar iske bawajood ham Aap (as) ko Ilm e Ghaib se mansoob nahi karte?"

Aap (as) ne farmaya:

"Quran mein hai: "Aur usne kaha jiske paas Kitab ka thoda Ilm tha ke mein Mulk e Saba ka Takht Aapki Aankh jhapakne se pehle Aap (as) ke paas le aaonga." (Naml: 40)

"Kya tum jaate ho uske paas kitna Ilm tha?"

"Uske paas Behre Akhzar mein se ek qatre ke barabar Ilm tha. Ab khud hi anadaza karen ke poori Kitab mein kitna Ilm hoga?"

"Ab batao jiske paas Kitab ka thoda sa Ilm tha, woh bada Aalim tha ya woh bada Aalim hai jiske paas poori Kitab ka Ilm ho?"

"Khuda ki Qasam! Poori Kitab ka Ilm hamare paas hai."

(Usoole Kaafi, Noorus Saqlain)

Fazilat Hazrat Ali (as):

Haris bin Sa'labah ne Saad bin Abi Waqas se kaha:

"Kya tu ne Ali (as) ke kuchh Fazail ka mushaheda kiya?"

Usne kaha:

"Ji haan, meine Ali (as) ke 5 Manaqeb ka mushaheda kiya aur Ali (as) ki Panchvi Fazail itni qeemti hai ke agar un mein se mujhe ek Fazilat bhi hasil hoti to woh mujhe surkh Oonton se bhi zyada Mehboob hoti. Hazrat Ali (as) ki Panchvi Fazilat ye hai ke Rasoole Khuda (saww) ne Abu Bakr ko Surah Bara'at ki Ayat de kar rawana kiya tha. Phir AanHazrat (saww) ne Hazrat Ali (as) ko rawana kiya aur unhone usse wo Ayat le li thi. Abu Bakr Rasoole Akram (saww) ke paas aaya aur arz kiya ke kya mere mutalliq Aasmaan se koi Hukm nazil hua hai?

Nabi Akram (saww) ne farmaya: "Nahi, baat ye hai ke meri taraf se woh Paigham pahuncha sakta hai jo mujh se ho."

Hazrat Ali (as) ne Roze Shura apni Khilafat ke istehqaq ke liye yehi Fazilat ki taraf ishara kiya. Jibrael (as) ne Rasoole Khuda (saww) se yehi farmaya tha ya to Aap khud jayen ya us ko bhejen jo Aap mein se ho. Is baat ka hazirin e Shura ne bhi iqrar kiya tha.

(Kitabul Khisaal, Noorus Saqlain)
