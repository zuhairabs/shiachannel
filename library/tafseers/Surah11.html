♦ QUR'AN KI AYAAT MOHKAM & MUFASSAL HAIN. ♦

As-salamu alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatohu

Start your Day by reading Quran: (1506)

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

الۤرۚ كِتَـٰبٌ أُحۡكِمَتۡ ءَایَـٰتُهُۥ ثُمَّ فُصِّلَتۡ مِن لَّدُنۡ حَكِیمٍ خَبِیرٍ.

"Alim Laam Raa! Ye woh Kitab hai, jiski Ayaat Mohkam banayi gayi hai. Phir Sahib e Hikmat ba khabar ki taraf se tafseel ke saath bayan ki gayi hain."

(Surah Hud: 1)

📚📖

👉 Tafseer:

Is Ayat mein الۤرۚ Mubtida (Subject) aur كِتَـٰبٌ iski Khabar (Predicate) hai, aur baqi Sifat (Adjective phrase) hai.

Mafhoom:

أُحۡكِمَتۡ ءَایَـٰتُهُ:

1. Quran mein Mohkam Ayaat ki tadaad zyada hai jo kabhi Mansookh (cancel) nahi hoti.

2. Iski Ayaat mein Khuda e Hakim ki taraf se Hikmat shamil hai.

3. Mohkam Ayaat Aqeeda Tauheed, Adl, Nabuwwat, Imamat aur Qayamat ke mafahim par hai to ye mansookh nahi ho sakti.

4. Qurani Ayaat mein koi Tanakhus o Tazaad (Defects & Contradictions) nahi hai aur ye Balaghat (Literature) mein akheri darje par faez hain jiski Kainaat mein misaal nahi milti.

فُصِّلَتۡ:

1. Ayaat ko Suraton aur Ayaat ko Ayaat ke tahet laya gaya hai.

2. Ayaat ko yake baad digar (one after another) bheja gaya hai.

3. Insaani zarooriyat ki tafseel hai.

4. Is mein Halaal o Haraam ko alag kar diya hai.

              ♦ ISTEGHFAR ♦

As-salamu alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatohu

Start your Day by reading Quran: (1507)

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

أَلَّا تَعۡبُدُوۤا۟ إِلَّا ٱللَّهَۚ إِنَّنِی لَكُم مِّنۡهُ نَذِیرࣱ وَبَشِیرࣱ *  وَأَنِ ٱسۡتَغۡفِرُوا۟ رَبَّكُمۡ ثُمَّ تُوبُوۤا۟ إِلَیۡهِ یُمَتِّعۡكُم مَّتَـٰعًا حَسَنًا إِلَىٰۤ أَجَلࣲ مُّسَمࣰّى وَیُؤۡتِ كُلَّ ذِی فَضۡلࣲ فَضۡلَهُۥۖ وَإِن تَوَلَّوۡا۟ فَإِنِّیۤ أَخَافُ عَلَیۡكُمۡ عَذَابَ یَوۡمࣲ كَبِیرٍ *  إِلَى ٱللَّهِ مَرۡجِعُكُمۡۖ وَهُوَ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَیۡءࣲ قَدِیرٌ.

"... Ke tum Allah ke kisiki Ibaadat na karo. Mein tumhare liye Uski jaanib se Daranewala aur Khushkhabari Denewala hun. Aur ye ke tum apne Parwardigar se Maghferat talab karo. Phir Uski taraf palat aao to Woh ek khaas muddat tak ke liye tumhe achcha Samaane Zeenat faraham karega. Aur har Sahibe Fazl ko uska Fazl ata karega lekin agar tum munh pherte ho to mein tumhare mutalliq ek bade haulnak Din ke Azaab se darta hun. Tumhe Khuda ki taraf palatna hai aur Woh har cheez par Qudrat rakhta hai."

(Surah Hud: 2 - 4)

📚📖

👉 Tafseer:

Ye Ayat guzishta Ayat ke zikr se paiwast hai.

Maqsad ye hai ke Kitab ko isliye Mohkam o Mufassal banaya gaya hai taake tum bas Allah ki hi Ibaadat karo. Kitab e Muqaddas ka ma hasl (outcome) hi hai ke Allah ke alawa kisiki Ibaadat na ki jaye aur Anbiya ki Be'sat (bhejna) aur unki Tableegh ka pahla maqsad bhi yehi hai. Rasool Akram (saww) Allah ki taraf se Nazir aur Bashir hai. Maqsad ye hai ke jo Kalma e Tauheed ka inkaar karte hain Rasoole Khuda (saww) unke liye Azaab ki khabar denewale hain aur jo log Tauheed e Khuda wandi ka iqraar karen aur Isteghfar aur Tauba karen to AanHazrat (saww) unke liye Bashir hain aur Aap (saww) ki Basharat ye hai ke agar tumne Isteghfar aur Tauba ke taqazon par amal kiya to Allah Ta'ala tumhe Duniya mein achha Samaane Zeenat faraham karega aur har shakhs ko uski Fazilat ke mutabiq darajat ata karega. Allah Ta'ala ne Duniyavi fayde ko lafz " *متاع*" (Mata') se tabeer kiya hai jiske maani hain thoda bahut fayda. Yani Duniyavi fayda Akherat ke muqabile mein intehai kam aur qaleel tareen hai.

🚩

Allah ka Usool ye hai ke har Sahibe Fazilat ko Fazilat ke Darajat ata karta hai.

Imam Baqir (as) se manqool hai isse murad Hazrat Ali (as) hain. Yani tamam Sahibane Fazilat mein Hazrat Ali (as) Aala o Arfa Shakhsiyat ke Malik hain.

(Tafseer Ali Ibne Ibrahim Qummi, Noorus Saqlain)

   ♦ MUNH CHHUPANE WALE ♦

As-salamu alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatohu

Start your Day by reading Quran: (1508)

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

أَلَاۤ إِنَّهُمۡ یَثۡنُونَ صُدُورَهُمۡ لِیَسۡتَخۡفُوا۟ مِنۡهُۚ أَلَا حِینَ یَسۡتَغۡشُونَ ثِیَابَهُمۡ یَعۡلَمُ مَا یُسِرُّونَ وَمَا یُعۡلِنُونَۚ إِنَّهُۥ عَلِیمُۢ بِذَاتِ ٱلصُّدُورِ.

"Agah raho! Ye log apne Seeno ko modte hain taake Usse chhup jayen. Agah raho! Jab ye Kapdon se apne aap ko dhanpte hain to jise woh chipa rahe hain aur jise zahir kar rahe hain use Allah khoob kaanta hai. Woh Seeno mein chhupe hue Raazon se waqif hai."

(Surah Hud: 5)

📚📖 😷

👉 Tafseer:

 Hazrat Imam Baqir (as):

"Rasoole Khuda (saww) Khana e Ka'aba ke Sahen mein baithe hote to us waqt Mushrekin Tawafe Ka'aba ke liye aate to woh apne Munh par Kapda dal lete the taake Rasoole Khuda (saww) unhe na pahchan saken."

(Rauza e Kaafi, Noorus Saqlain)

🕋

Jab Rasoole Khuda (saww) Hazrat Ali (as) ki koi Fazilat bayan karte to Munafiq apne Kapde sametne lag jate the. Chunanche Allah ne is Ayat mein unki isi haalat ko bayan kiya hai.

(Tafseer Ali Ibne Ibrahim Qummi, Noorus Saqlain)

😷

Ayat ka mafhoom ye hai ke tum agar kisi ko sataate ho ya koi aur Gunah ka irtikaab karte ho to aisa hota hai ke to usse Munh churate hai, Munh dhak kar chalte ho. Aapne kisi ka Qarz ada nahi kar ra rahe to usse Munh chhupa kar chaloge. Lekin Allah sab kuchh dekhta hai usse Munh nahi chhupa sakte, Woh tumhare Man ki baat Jaanta hai.

😷

Dekha jaye to koi bhi Insaan Masoom nahi hai. Aajkal ye Corona ki Waba ne ham sab ko aisi haalat mein dal diya hai ke ham apna Munh chhupaye chhupaye phir rahe hain. Mumkin hai ye ham par Azaab ho ya hamara Imtihaan liya jaa raha hai. Nafsa nafsi ka Aalam hai. Har Insaan Bargah e Khuda wandi ke saamne bhooka pyasa khada hai, har Insaan is baat ka Muntazir hai kab koi Allah ko pyara ho jaye. Aaj Duniya ki badi badi taqat ne Khuda ke saamne ghutne tek diye hai. Bahut Ghuroor tha Insaan ko, Star War karna chahta tha, Nuclear Bomb par pura bharosa tha, sab chakna choor ho gaya. Ek na dikhne wale Virus se haar gaya.

Aisi haalat mein Momin siwai Khuda ke saamne tasleem (surrender) hone ke kuchh nahi kar sakte.
