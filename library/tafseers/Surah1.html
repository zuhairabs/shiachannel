♦ _*SAB'UN MASANI SURAH AL HAMD*_ ♦

As-salamu alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatohu

Start your Day by reading Quran: (928)

*بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ*

*بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ * الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ * الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ * مَالِكِ يَوْمِ الدِّينِ * إِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وَإِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِينُ * اهْدِنَا الصِّرَاطَ الْمُسْتَقِيمَ * صِرَاطَ الَّذِينَ أَنْعَمْتَ عَلَيْهِمْ غَيْرِ الْمَغْضُوبِ عَلَيْهِمْ وَلَا الضَّالِّينَ*

_"Tamam Ta'reefen Allah ke liye makhsoos hain, jo tamam jahanon ka Palnewala hai, Bada Meherban aur Nihayat Rahem wala, Roze Jaza ka Maalik hai. Khudaya! Ham Teri hi Ibaadat karte hain aur sirf Tujh se hi Madad chahte hain. Tu Hamko Seedhe Raaste par sabit qadam rakh, unka Raasta jinhen Tu ne Apni Ne'mat ata' ki hai, na  unka Raasta jin par Tera Ghazab dhaya gaya hai aur na hi Gumrahon ka."_
(Surah Fatiha: 1 - 7)

📚📖
👉 _*Tafseer:*_
Aaj se ham Surah Al Hamd ki Tafseer shuru kar rahe hain.

Is Surah ki Bismillah samit 7 Ayaat hain jaisa ke is Ayat mein iska tarruf is tarah kiya hai:
*وَلَقَدْ آتَيْنَاكَ سَبْعًا مِنَ الْمَثَانِي وَالْقُرْآنَ الْعَظِيمَ*

_"Hamne Aapko (Saba' Masani) 7 Aayaton par mushtamil Surah Hamd ata kiya jo do martaba nazil kiya gaya aur Quran Azeem bhi enayat farmaya gaya."_
(Surah Al-Hijr 87)

Ye Surah do martaba nazil hua Makkah aur Madina mein, isliye Namaz mein do bar padha jata hai. Is Surah ko Quran ke muqabil qarar diya gaya hai.

Ahle Sunnat ke mutabiq chunke Bismillah ko Surah ka juz nahi maante isliye woh Surah mein count nahi hoti. Aksar aap aam printed Qur'an mein 6 Ayaat payenge. Jahan akheri Ayat par no.7 likha hota hai, aur no. 6 beech Ayat mein laga diya gaya hota hai ya ghaib rahta hai.
Ahle Bait (as) ki taa'leemat ke mutabiq Bismillah Surah ka juz hai aur is tarah 7 Ayaat Bismillah ko mila kar hoti hain. Jaise baaz doosri Aayaton mein bhi Bismillah Ayat ka juz hai.

_"Tamam Ta'reefen Allah ke liye makhsoos hain, jo tamam jahanon ka Palnewala hai, Bada Meherban aur Nihayat Rahem wala, Roze Jaza ka Maalik hai. Khudaya! 📚📖
👉 _*Tafseer:*_ (Cont'd...)
*وَلَقَدْ آتَيْنَاكَ سَبْعًا مِنَ الْمَثَانِي وَالْقُرْآنَ الْعَظِيمَ*

_"Hamne Aapko (Saba' Masani) 7 Aayaton par mushtamil Surah Hamd ata kiya jo do martaba nazil kiya gaya aur Quran Azeem bhi enayat farmaya gaya."_
(Surah Al-Hijr 87)

Maujooda Qur'an jo aam Musalman padhte hain us mein us mein 6 Ayaat hain aur akheri Ayat par no.7 likha hota hai, iski wajah ye hai ke Bismillah ko Quran ki Ayat nahi samajhte, jabke har cheez ki shurua'at Bismillah se hoti hai.
Kuchh Rivayaat mulaheza farmayen:
1. Imam Sadiq (as) se jab poochha gaya ke Al Hamd ki ibteda Bismillah se ki jaye to farmaya 'haan'.
(Kaafi, Vol 3, P-312)

2. 'Bismillah Surah Hamd ka juz hai.'
(Sunni Tafseer Al Munaar, Vol 1, P-39,40)

3. Hazrat Ali (as) se ek shakhs ne poochha ke 'Sab'o Masani' kiya hai?
Aap (as) ne farmaya:
_"Surah Al Hamd."_
Poochha ke Surah Al Hamd ki to sirf 6 Ayaten hain?
Aap (as) ne farmaya:
_"Bismillahir Rahmanir Rahim bhi uski ek Ayat hai."_
(Sunni Book 'Al Ittefaq' by Darul Qutni)

Ahle Sunnat ke mashhur Muhaddis Bahqi Sanad e Sahih ke saath naql karte hain:
_"Shaitan sifat ashkhas ne Qur'an ki bahut badi Ayat "Bismillahir Rahmanir Rahim" ko chura liya hai."_
(Ye is taraf ishara hai ke isko is Surah ke shuru mein nahi padha jaata.)
(Bahqi, Vol 2, P-50)

'Ek din Moaviya ne apni hukumat ke zamane mein Namaz ba Jamat mein Bismillah na padhi to Namaz ke baad Muhajir o Ansaar ke ek giroh ne pukar kar kaha: _"اشرقت ام نسيت kiya Moaviya ne Bismillah ko chura liya hai ya bhool gaya hai?"_
(Bahqi, Vol 2, P-49, Mustadrak e Hakim, Part 1, P-233)

Ho sakta hai ye bhi tassub ka shikar ho gayi kyonki Hazrat Ali (as) ki mashhur rivayat jis mein unhone ne farmaya "Mein Ba e Bismillah ka Nuqta hun."  Kyonki ye Ayat Hazrat Ali (as) se mansoob hai.

Imam Sadiq (a.s.)  
"Bismillah Al-Rahman Al-Raheem" ke bare mein sawal kiya.
Imam (a.s.) ne farmaya:
Ba با is for Glory of Allah Allah ki Tasbeeh,*
Seen س is for Splendor of Allah Allah ki Shaan,*
Meem م is for Kingdom of Allah, Allah ka Mulk*

Phir “Allah” se ma’ani poochhe.
Imam (a.s.) ne farmaya:
*Alif أ stands for Blessings of our Wilayat on His creation. (Alif se Uski Vilayat Uski Makhlooq par.)
