*بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ*

*یَسۡـَٔلُونَكَ عَنِ ٱلۡأَنفَالِۖ قُلِ ٱلۡأَنفَالُ لِلَّهِ وَٱلرَّسُولِۖ فَٱتَّقُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ وَأَصۡلِحُوا۟ ذَاتَ بَیۡنِكُمۡۖ وَأَطِیعُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ وَرَسُولَهُۥۤ إِن كُنتُم مُّؤۡمِنِینَ.*

         ♦ _*ANFAAL (BOOTY)*_ ♦

As-salamu alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatohu

Start your Day by reading Quran: (1302)

*بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ*

*یَسۡـَٔلُونَكَ عَنِ ٱلۡأَنفَالِۖ قُلِ ٱلۡأَنفَالُ لِلَّهِ وَٱلرَّسُولِۖ فَٱتَّقُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ وَأَصۡلِحُوا۟ ذَاتَ بَیۡنِكُمۡۖ وَأَطِیعُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ وَرَسُولَهُۥۤ إِن كُنتُم مُّؤۡمِنِینَ*

_"(Aye Nabi! (saww)) Log Aap (saww) se Maal e Ghanimat (War Booty) ke mutalliq daryaft karte hain. Aap kah den ke Maal e Ghanimat Khuda aur Rasool ka hai. Tum Khuda se darte raho aur bahami Sulah Safai qayem rakho aur agar tum Momin ho to Khuda aur Uske Rasool ki ita'at karo."_

(Surah Anfaal: 1)

----------------------------------------------

_*MORAL OF THE DAY ....!!!!!!!!*_

🙇 📚📖

👉 _*Tafseer:*_

Lafz *"انفال"* lafz *نَفَل* ki jama (plural) hai. Aur kisi bhi izafi cheez ko 'Nafal' kaha jata hai, isliye Mustahabbi Namazon ko Nafal kaha jata hai. Anfaal ko *"فيئ - Fai"* ke meaning mein bhi isyemal kiya jata hai yaani jiska koi Maalik na ho, maslan Pahad ki chotiyon, Waadiyon, ujde hue Shaher, be Waris ka Tarka jiska koi Waaris ya Maalik na ho use Maal e Anfaal kahte hain.

Jang ka asl maqsd Dushman ki tabahi o khatma hota hai lekin ek giroh maghloob hone par jiska Dushman ke Maal lar qabza ho jata hai, he izaafi hota hai, isliye ise 'Anfaal' kahte hain.

💰

Riwayat mein hai:

Khums 5 cheezob par Wajib hai:

(1) Khazana o Dafina

(2) Ma'adin (Mines)

(3) Darya aur Samandar ki Ghawasi se jo Maal hasil ho.

(4) Dushman se Jang ke baad hasil hone wala Maal.

(5) Aur jo Maal ya Ilaaqa Jang ke baghair hasil ho aur Sulah pasandi se hasil ho wo Maal Khuda aur Rasool ka hai. Is mein Wadiyon ke Butoon, Pahad ki chotiyan aur Banjar be Waris Zameenen shamil hain."

(Tehzeebul Ahkam, Usoole Kafi, Noorus Saqlain)

💰

Imam Baqir (as):

_"Anfaal izaafi Maal ka doosra naam hai aur Surah Anfaal mein ek Hukm aisa bhi hai jis par logon ne Amal Tark karke apne liye badnaami hasil ki hai."_

(Noorus Saqlain)

Is Hadis mein is Amr ki taraf ishara hai ke Surah Anfaal mein Ayat e Khums maujood hai aur is mein " Zul Qurba" ka hissa bhi mazkoor hai. Musalmano ne Rasoole Khuda (saww) ke Qarabat Daron ko Khums se mehroom karke apne liye badnaami hasil ki hai.

Imam Sadiq (as):

_"Ham woh log hain jinki ita'at Khuda ne Farz ki hai. Hamare liye "Anfaal" aur "Safwul Maal" hai."_

(Noorus Saqlain)

💰

Imam Sadiq (as):

_"Anfaal' se murad ghair Abaad Shaher hain, jinke rahne wale unhe chhod kar kahin chale gaye hon. Aisi Jaidaad Khuda aur Rasool ki hai Milkiyat hai. Baadshahon ki makhsoos Jaidaaden bhi Imam ki Milkiyat hain. Tamam weeran aur Banjar Zameenen aur aisi Zameenen jinke husool ke liye Oont aur Ghode na daudaye gaye hon aur aisi Zameen jo kisi ki Milkiyat na ho, iske alawa Ma'aden (Mines) aur jis marne wale ka koi Waaris na ho to aisa tamam Amwaal "Anfaal" mein shamil hain."_

(Tafseer Ali Ibne Ibrahim Qummi, Noorus Saqlain)

🤲🏻

_"Aapke khilaf Jang aur Fitno ki dhool jhonk di, aur Aapke khilaf Keeno ki Talwaren Niyaam se nikal lin, Ehteram ke Pardo ko chaak kar diya, *Aapke Khums se Sharaab khareedi.*...."_

(Ziyarat e Jame - Mafateeh)

♦ _*MAAL-E-GHANIMAT, SULAH*_ ♦

As-salamu alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatohu

Start your Day by reading Quran: (1303)

*بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ*

*یَسۡـَٔلُونَكَ عَنِ ٱلۡأَنفَالِۖ قُلِ ٱلۡأَنفَالُ لِلَّهِ وَٱلرَّسُولِۖ فَٱتَّقُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ وَأَصۡلِحُوا۟ ذَاتَ بَیۡنِكُمۡۖ وَأَطِیعُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ وَرَسُولَهُۥۤ إِن كُنتُم مُّؤۡمِنِینَ*

_"(Aye Nabi! (saww)) Log Aap (saww) se Maal e Ghanimat (War Booty) ke mutalliq daryaft karte hain. Aap kah den ke Maal e Ghanimat Khuda aur Rasool ka hai. Tum Khuda se darte raho aur bahami Sulah Safai qayem rakho aur agar tum Momin ho to Khuda aur Uske Rasool ki ita'at karo."_

(Surah Anfaal: 1)

📚📖

👉 _*Tafseer:*_

Ye Ayat Jang e Badr se mutalliq hai. Badr ki Tafseer ham Surah Baqarah: 217 ke zel mein pesh kar chuke hain. Jang e Badr ki tafseel kaafi Kitabon darj hai. Detail ki yahan gunjaish nahi hai.

⚔ 💰 _*Shane Nuzool:*_

Jange Badr mein Mujahideen ke 3 Giroh ban gaye the. Ek AanHazrat (saww) ki muhafizat ke liye, doosra Dushman ko maar bhagane ke liye aur Teesre woh the jinhone Maal e Ghanimat jama kiya. Jang ke khatme ke baad Maale Ghanimat par ikhtelaf ho gaya. Har koi us par apni Milkiyat ka dawa karne laga. Jab jhagda zyada badha to Rasoole Akram (saww) par ye Ayat (8:1) nazil hui. Aur is Ayat ke baad Khums ki Ayat (8:41) nazil hui. Chunanche is Ayat ke nuzool ke baad AanHazrat (saww) ne is Maal e Ghanimat mein se Panchva hissa alaihda kar liya aur baqi 4 hisse Mujahideen mein taqseem kar diye.

(Noorus Saqlain)

💰

Imam Sadiq (as):

_"Jang e Badr ka Maal e Ghanimat Paighambar (saww) se makhsoos tha lekin Aapne Bakhsish ke taur par use Lashkare Islam mein taqseem kar diya."_

(Kanzul Irfaan, Vol 1, P-254)

Usne un se kaha ke tumhe maloom hona chahiye ke ye mera Maal nahi tha, balke Imam (as) ne mujhe hukm de rakha hai ke aise mauqe par Maal e Imam (Sahme Imam) se istefada karte hue Ashaab ke darmiyan Sulah karwa do.

(Usoole Kaafi, Babe Islah, H-1 & 2)

(Ramazan Dua: Misbaah - Kaf'ami, Baladul Ameen, Mafateeh)

       ♦ _*MOMIN KI SIFAAT*_ ♦

.

As-salamu alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatohu

Start your Day by reading Quran: (1304)

*بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ*

*إِنَّمَا ٱلۡمُؤۡمِنُونَ ٱلَّذِینَ إِذَا ذُكِرَ ٱللَّهُ وَجِلَتۡ قُلُوبُهُمۡ وَإِذَا تُلِیَتۡ عَلَیۡهِمۡ ءَایَـٰتُهُۥ زَادَتۡهُمۡ إِیمَـٰنࣰا وَعَلَىٰ رَبِّهِمۡ یَتَوَكَّلُونَ ۝  ٱلَّذِینَ یُقِیمُونَ ٱلصَّلَوٰةَ وَمِمَّا رَزَقۡنَـٰهُمۡ یُنفِقُونَ ۝  أُو۟لَـٰۤىِٕكَ هُمُ ٱلۡمُؤۡمِنُونَ حَقࣰّاۚ لَّهُمۡ دَرَجَـٰتٌ عِندَ رَبِّهِمۡ وَمَغۡفِرَةࣱ وَرِزۡقࣱ كَرِیمࣱ.*

_"Momin to bas woh log hain ke jab Khuda ka Zikr kiya jaye to unke Dil kaanp uthen aur jab un par Ayaat ki Tilawat ki jaye to unke Imaan mein izaafa ho jaye aur apne Rab par Bharosa rakhte hain. Woh jo Namaz qayem karte hain aur Hamne jo kuchh unko diya hai us mein se woh kharch karte hain. Yehi log Haqeeqi Momin hain. Unke liye unke Rab ke paas Darajaat, Bakhshish aur Izzat ki Rozi hai."_

(Surah Anfaal: 2 - 4)

----------------------------------------------

_*MORAL OF THE DAY ....!!!!!!!!*_

🙇 📚📖

👉 _*Tafseer:*_

In Ayaat mein Allah Ta'ala ne Momin ki 5 Imtiyazi Sifaat ki taraf ishara kiya hai jin mein se 3 Ruhaani pahlu rakhti hain aur do kharji pahlu rakhti hain.

(1) Ehsaas e Zimmedari

(2) Imaan ka Takamul aur irteqa.

(3) Tawakkal.

(4) Khuda se irtebat.

(5) Khalqe Khuda se talluq.

👉🏻

Irshad hota hai Mo'meneen sirf woh log hain ke jab bhi Khuda ka Naam liya jaye to unke Dil Ehsaas e Mas'ooliyat (Responsibility) se Uski Bargah mein dhadakne lagte hain. *وجل* Khauf aur Darr ki us kaifiyat ko kahte hain jo Insaan ko do mein se ek wajah se lahaq hoti hai. Ya to ye ke Zimmedariyon ke Ehsaas ki wajah se ya ye ke mein ek La Mutanahi Khuda aur Uski Azmat ki wajah se.

🍁

Imam Ali (as):

_"Khauf e Khuda se Momin ka rona uske aa'nkho'n ki thhandak hai."_

(Hadiyat ush Shia, p44)

☘

Imam Ali (as):

_"Momin ka chiraag hamare Haq ki maarefat hai aur badtareen andhaapan hamare fazl se aa'nkhein band kar lena hai."_

(Aqwaal e Masoomeen (as), p167)

          ♦ _*JANG E BADR*_ ♦

.

As-salamu alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatohu

Start your Day by reading Quran: (1305)

*بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ*

*كَمَاۤ أَخۡرَجَكَ رَبُّكَ مِنۢ بَیۡتِكَ بِٱلۡحَقِّ وَإِنَّ فَرِیقࣰا مِّنَ ٱلۡمُؤۡمِنِینَ لَكَـٰرِهُونَ *  یُجَـٰدِلُونَكَ فِی ٱلۡحَقِّ بَعۡدَ مَا تَبَیَّنَ كَأَنَّمَا یُسَاقُونَ إِلَى ٱلۡمَوۡتِ وَهُمۡ یَنظُرُونَ*

_"Jis tarah se tumhare Parwardigar ne tumko Haq ke saath tumhare Ghar se nikala jabke Ahle Imaan giroh isse na khush tha. Woh Haq ke zahir hone ke baad Aap se yun jhagadte the goya unhe Maut ki taraf dhakela jaa raha ho aur woh use dekh rahe hon."_

(Surah Anfaal: 5 - 6)

📚📖

👉 _*Tafseer:*_

Ye Ayat bhi Jang e Badr ki taraf ishara kar rahi hai. Khuchh naye naye Musalman Maal e Ghanimat ki taqseem ki kaifiyat se naraaz the jab unhe Maslehate Khuda wandi ka ilm nahi tha. Jang aur uski fatehyabi sab Allah ki taeed par waqe hui. Ye Jang Haq ke liye thi jahan Haq o Baatil aamne saamne the. Is mein hame ye sabaq milta hai ke Allah ke Hukm par hame har waqt apna Sar Tasleem rakhna chahiye.

Ye Tasleem o Raza hame Karbala ki Jang mein dikhayi deti hai jahan kisi ko ye ehsaas nahi tha ke hame Jang mein jhonka jaa raha hai, ya Maut ki taraf dhakela ja raha hai. Wahan to bas unke liye Maut Shahed se zyada meethi thi, Imam ke saamne agar bar zinda kiye jayen to Hukm e Imam ke saamne Sar khum kiye hue Shaheed hone ke kiye tayyar the.

⚜⚜⚜⚜⚜⚜⚜⚜⚜⚜

Detail ke liye Kitaben refer karen.

(Tafseer Ali Ibne Ibrahim Qummi, Noorus Saqlain)

Start your Day by reading Quran: (1336)

*بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ*

*وَلَا تَكُونُوا۟ كَٱلَّذِینَ خَرَجُوا۟ مِن دِیَـٰرِهِم بَطَرࣰا وَرِئَاۤءَ ٱلنَّاسِ وَیَصُدُّونَ عَن سَبِیلِ ٱللَّهِۚ وَٱللَّهُ بِمَا یَعۡمَلُونَ مُحِیطࣱ*

_"Aur un logon ki tarah se mat bano jo apne gharon se itraate hue aur logon ke dikhane ke liye nikale aur unki ravish ye thi ke ke woh Allah ke Raaste se rok rahe the jabke Allah unke tamam Aamaal ka ahata kiye hue hai."_

(Surah Anfal: 47)

(Surah Baqarah: 114)

📚📖 🕋🕌

👉 _*Tafseer:*_

Is Ayat ki tafseer ye hai ke Yahoodi Baitul Muqaddas mein nahi karne dete.

(Kitab Asbabun Nuzool)

🕌 Doosri Tafseer ye hai:

Imam Sadiq (as):

_"Jab Quraish ne Rasoole Khuda (saww) ko Masjidul Haram aur dukhool e Makkah se mana kiya to unke mutalliq ye Ayat nazil hui. (2:114)"_

(Majmaul Bayan, Noorus Saqlain)

🕌

Hazrat Ali (as):

_"Is Ayat mein har woh shakhs dakhil hai jo kisi bhi shakhs ko Zameen par Zikr e Khuda se mana kare kyonki Rasoole Khuda (saww) ka irshad hai:_

*جعلت لى الأرض مسجدا و ترابها طهورا.*

_"Mere liye tamam Zameen jo Masjid aur uski khaak ko Pakeezgi ka zariya banaya gaya hai."_

(Noorus Saqlain)

🕋🕌

Mecca mein Ka'abe ke atraaf Masjidul Haram hai jahan Musalman Ibaadaten wa Zikre Khuda kiya karte the lekin Kuffar e Quraish Rasoole Khuda (saww) aur unke Ashaab ko aziyyat dene lage yahan tak ke wahan Ibaadat bhi nahi karne dete the. Pas Rasoole Khuda (saww) ne Hijrat ki aur Medina 12th Rabiul Awwal ko tashreef laye aur Medine ke bahar Aap (saww) ne pahli Masjid 'Masjid e Quba' tameer ki. Aap (saww) Hazrat Ali (as) ke pahunnchne tak wahin qayam kiya.

Kuffar e Quraish ne jis tarah pahle Masjidul Haram mein pareshaniyan paida ki, usi ko Munafeqeen ne follow kiya. Karbala ke waqae (Oct 680 AD) ke baad "Jange Hurra" hua (26 Aug 683 AD) . Medina taraaj hua. Aurten mehfooz nahi rahi. Yazeed ki 12000 Shami faujon ne Medina par hamla kiya jiska Sardar Muslim bin Uqba Al Murri tha. Uqba Ka'abe ki taraf chala lekin pahle hi mar gaya. Phir Husain bin Numayr Al Sakuni ki Sardari mein Ka'abe ki behurmati ki gayi. Use mein ghode bandhe gaye. Ibne Zubair se muqabala hua. 31st Oct 683 AD ke din Ka'aba jalaya gaya. Yazid ke marne tak Mecca ko uski faujon ne ghere rakha aur kafi kharabiyan ki. 3 Saal tak Masjidul Haram mein Namaz nahi padhi gayi. Islam ko khatm karne ki poori koshish ki gayi. Ye sab tarikh ki Kitabon mein maujood hai, yahan khulase ke taur par pesh kiya. Lekin phir bhi aaj Musalman inki safaiyan pesh karta aur inke liye soft corner rakhta hai. Allah ne aise hi logon ko sab se bada Zalim thahraya hai.
